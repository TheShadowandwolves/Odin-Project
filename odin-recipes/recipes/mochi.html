Easy Mochi Recipe

Description:

This easy mochi recipe is a delightful treat that combines chewy rice cake with a sweet filling. Perfect for satisfying your dessert cravings!


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour (mochiko)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • Cornstarch or potato starch (for dusting)
  • Sweet filling of your choice (e.g., red bean paste, ice cream, fruit)

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine glutinous rice flour and sugar. Gradually add water while stirring until the mixture is smooth and free of lumps.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, leaving a small vent for steam to escape. Microwave on high for 1 minute. Remove and stir the mixture. Repeat this process two more times, microwaving for 1 minute and stirring each time, until the mochi is thick and slightly translucent.
  3. Dust a clean surface with cornstarch or potato starch. Carefully transfer the hot mochi onto the dusted surface. Be cautious as it will be very hot.
  4. Let the mochi cool for a few minutes until it is safe to handle. Once cooled, divide the mochi into small portions (about 8-10 pieces).
  5. Flatten each portion into a round disc using your hands or a rolling pin dusted with starch. Place a small amount of your chosen filling in the center of each disc.
  6. Carefully fold the edges of the mochi over the filling and pinch to seal. Shape into a ball or desired shape.
  7. Dust off any excess starch and serve immediately, or store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 1 day. Enjoy your homemade mochi!
